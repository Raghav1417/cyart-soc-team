A threat hunting exercise was conducted to detect misuse of valid accounts based on MITRE ATT&CK technique T1078. The hypothesis assumed that attackers may leverage legitimate credentials to gain privileged access. Log analysis in Wazuh identified authentication-related events such as rule ID 5502, indicating sudo-based privilege usage by user “raghav”. Windows logs were also analyzed, though no Event ID 4672 was observed. Threat intelligence from AlienVault OTX identified IPs associated with brute-force and scanning activity. Velociraptor was used to analyze endpoint processes. Correlation of all data suggests possible misuse of valid credentials. Continuous monitoring and access control are recommended.
