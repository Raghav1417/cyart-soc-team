A simulated attack was conducted using Kali Linux against a vulnerable Metasploitable system. Suspicious activities such as SSH login attempts and privilege escalation were detected in Wazuh logs. These activities were mapped to MITRE ATT&CK techniques including T1548.003 and T1078. Immediate containment was performed by blocking the attacker IP using CrowdSec. The incident was escalated for further investigation and monitoring improvements.
